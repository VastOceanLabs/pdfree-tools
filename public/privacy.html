1. Data We Collect

Privacy-First Approach

We collect minimal data to provide our service:

Files You Upload

When you use our PDF tools, we temporarily process your files to provide the requested service (convert, merge, split, etc.). We do not permanently store, read, or analyze the content of your files.

Technical Information

We collect basic technical information to ensure our service works properly:

  • IP address (for rate limiting and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and version (for compatibility)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop for optimization)
  • File processing statistics (file size, processing time)

What We DON'T Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Email addresses or personal contact information
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • File content or metadata
  • Tracking cookies for advertising
  • Personal identifying information

2. File Processing & Storage

Automatic 1-Hour Deletion

All uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted after approximately 1 hour, regardless of whether you download the processed result.

Technical Infrastructure

Our file processing infrastructure is designed for privacy and security:

Storage Location: Cloudflare R2 (Global Edge Network)
Processing: Client-side when possible, secure cloud workers for large files
Encryption: Files encrypted at rest using AES-256
Access: Only automated processing systems, no human access
Deletion: Automated deletion within approximately 1 hour

Cloudflare R2 Storage Regions

Your files may be temporarily stored in any of Cloudflare's global data centers for optimal performance. Primary regions include:

  • United States (multiple locations)
  • European Union (GDPR compliant facilities)
  • Asia-Pacific (Australia, Singapore, Japan)
  • Other Cloudflare edge locations as needed for performance

All storage locations maintain the same 1-hour deletion policy and encryption standards.

Processing Methods

Client-Side Processing (Preferred)

When possible, we process files directly in your browser using JavaScript libraries. This means:

  • Maximum privacy protection
  • Faster processing times
  • Reduced server load
  • Enhanced user experience

Server-Side Processing (When Necessary)

For large files or complex operations, we use secure cloud processing:

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS 1.3)
  • Processing in isolated containers
  • No logging of file content
  • Immediate deletion after processing

3. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Your Rights Under GDPR (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you have these rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data we process
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for specific purposes

Your Rights Under CCPA (California Users)

If you're a California resident, you have these additional rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices
Easy Compliance

Since we don't store your files or require accounts, most requests are automatically fulfilled by our 1-hour deletion policy. For other inquiries, contact us below.

4. Cookies & Tracking

No Advertising Trackers

Cookie-less Analytics

6. Data Security

Security Measures

We protect your data with industry-standard security:

  • Encryption in Transit: HTTPS/TLS 1.3 for all connections
  • Encryption at Rest: AES-256 encryption for stored files
  • Access Controls: No human access to user files
  • Rate Limiting: Prevents abuse and ensures fair usage
  • Regular Security Audits: Automated and manual security testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hardened servers and containers

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident:

  • We'll assess the scope within 24 hours
  • Affected users will be notified within 72 hours
  • We'll provide clear information about what happened
  • Regulatory authorities will be notified as required
